Tips for Working with AI

‚ñº

The Layer and Loop Method (for Refine & Verify tasks)

1
Layer
Add your judgement over the AI draft. Check facts, adjust tone, remove anything you wouldn't say.
2
Loop
Run through once more to tighten accuracy and consistency.
3
Stop
Then stop. Not endlessly refining. Just enough to keep quality where it needs to be.

Briefing AI effectively

  • Give context: who's it for, what's the purpose
  • Specify format: length, structure, tone
  • Share examples of what "good" looks like
  • State constraints: what to avoid, what must be included

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Using AI output without reading it properly
  • Assuming AI knows your context or preferences
  • Endlessly refining instead of shipping
  • Using AI for tasks where trust is the product

When to override this tool

  • Your gut says a task needs more care than suggested
  • The specific recipient changes the stakes
  • You're in a crisis or sensitive period
  • Legal or regulatory requirements apply

Building AI into your workflow

  • Start with Delegate tasks to build confidence
  • Create templates for recurring tasks
  • Save prompts that work well for reuse
  • Review and adjust your matrix quarterly
